Accomodation

Here's a broad description of Indy's areas:


Downtown Indy: This is where the city center is and also where we live. The most happening part of downtown is Mass Ave where you can find several restaurants and craft cocktail bars as well as a lot of hotels and airbnbs. We have a room block at Bottleworks Hotel for June 23rd under the name "Acharya-Chanana".


Broad Ripple has a free-spirited vibe, with laid-back cafes, global eateries, lively brewpubs and happening clubs. The popular Monon Trail runs through this area.

Fountain Square Fountain Square is a quirky neighborhood with several galleries, a vintage duckpin bowling alley, laid-back eateries, offbeat nightlife including music cafes and burlesque clubs, craft cocktail bars. This is also near where a lot of HGTV shows are filmed.

Brownsburg, Whitestown, Avon: These are the suburbs of Indianapolis close to where the reception dinner is. If you're over 40, we recommend staying here.

Carmel, Zionsville: Rich suburbs of Indy that are far from the venue. They have some cute stuff.

Please text us if you have any specific questions about where to stay.

Transportation

We like to say everything in and around Indianapolis is ~20 mins away from each other so please rent a car.
